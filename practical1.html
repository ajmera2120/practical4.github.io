About Fullstack Web Development

Web development refers in general to the tasks associated with developing websites for hosting via intranet or internet.

The web development process includes web design, web content development, client-side/server-side scripting and network security configuration, among other tasks.

In a broader sense, web development encompasses all the actions, updates, and operations required to build, maintain and manage a website to ensure its performance, user experience, and speed are optimal.
